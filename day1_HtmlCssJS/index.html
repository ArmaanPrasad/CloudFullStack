Valorant VCT

The VALORANT Champions Tour (VCT) is the official global esports circuit for VALORANT, organized by Riot Games. It brings together the world's best professional teams through regional leagues in Americas, EMEA, Pacific, and China, where they compete to qualify for international events such as Masters and the season-ending Champions tournament. VCT is known for its intense tactical gameplay, high mechanical skill, and strategic team coordination, making it one of the fastest-growing esports ecosystems in the world. Throughout the season, teams earn qualification and championship points based on their performances, with the ultimate goal of becoming the VALORANT World Champion at Champions. Since its launch in 2021, the VCT has helped establish VALORANT as a premier competitive esport, attracting millions of viewers worldwide through thrilling matches, passionate fanbases, and iconic teams and players.
